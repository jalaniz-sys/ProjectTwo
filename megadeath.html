About Megadeath:

  • The band was formed in 1983 by a former member of Metallica, Dave Mustaine.
  • The album that made its debut was Killing Is My Business...And Business Is Good!
  • This album did not just receive recognition from the metal community but also the mainstream music community for its unique thrash metal/speed metal elements.
  • The success of this album made it possible for the band to delve deeper in the genre with, Peace Sells...but whos buying.
  • Megadeath alongside Metallica, Anthrax, and Slayer were all pioneers and regarded as the "Big Four" for American thrash metal.

About the Album:

  • Album was released in 1986 with Capital Records.
  • This album wanted to break the stereotype of heavy metal writing being done by people of lower intellect. This can be reflected in Peace Sells. Not only this but also how the flaw in the social structure found in the American Dream. Its a call for reformation
  • The Conjuring talks about a satanic ritual
  • Devils Island refers to a French Penal colony that was used by the country to get rid of political rivals.
  • Good Mourning/Black Friday explores supernatural elements.
  • My Last Words is about a game of Russian Roulette and the feeling of going through it.

Participating Members of Album:
